SMS Notification Policy

Messaging terms, consent, and opt-out information

Purpose

This phone number is used solely to send automated personal notification messages. These notifications alert the account holder when emails are received from specific pre-approved senders. No marketing, promotional, or commercial messages are sent from this number.

Consent

Messages are sent only to the account holder who has configured and opted in to receive these notifications. No third parties receive messages from this number. Consent is established through the account holder's own setup and configuration of the notification service.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on the volume of incoming emails from watched senders. Typically, this results in a small number of messages per day. Standard message and data rates may apply.

Opt-Out

You can stop receiving messages at any time.

Reply STOP to unsubscribe from all notifications.

Reply HELP for assistance or more information.

Privacy

No personal data is shared with third parties. Phone numbers are not sold, rented, or disclosed. Message content is limited to sender name, email subject line, and a brief preview.
